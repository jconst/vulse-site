How To Attach

This is a guide on how to attach your phone to your instrument.

To take full advantage of Vulse's interactive features, we recommend using a micro-suction phone case to temporarily affix your device to your instrument.

We recommend Mega Tiny Corp's Anti-Gravity Case. To see why it was chosen, other options, and general advice, follow the Attachment Guide link.
